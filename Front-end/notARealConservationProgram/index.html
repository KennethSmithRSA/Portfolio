Not a real Conservation programe is an inititive that aims to provide information on South Africa's many biomes, it's fauna, flora, and geology.

Our goals?

Our goals are not only to provide accurate and up-to-date information but to make that information accessable in one easy to navigate and unique website.

Are we correct?

Not a real Conservation program prides itself on collecting accurate information from accredited sources, all our sources are cites in text for easy of access.

What are biomes?

Simply put a biome is an area that is described based on the most dominant plant forms as well as the prevailing climatic conditions. Climatic conditions such as humidity, average rainfall, and temperature influences plant growth and the physiognomy (the apperance) of the all species that inhabit a biome. All biomes have a characteristic set of plant and animal species. Mucina and Rutherford (2006)
